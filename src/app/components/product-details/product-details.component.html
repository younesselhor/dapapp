BILT For Summer Heat

The Sprint Gloves from BILT are an aggressive, short cuff glove that are ready to transport you from apex to apex in the warmer months. Perforation along the back of the hand and fingers increase airflow. The unlined, full leather construction balances protection with uninterrupted communication between rider and machine.

BILT To Protect

Suede overlays add reinforcement at the palm and grip at the control interface the forward portion of the palm, index, and middle fingers. Molded TPR knuckle and finger sliders are also vented to boost airflow further. Ready for the torquiest machines, a hook and loop closure wraps the wrist and delivers a snug, secure fit.
